Turn your vision into action: our visual management turns ideas into reality with clarity and precision!

Our online platform will help you and your team see the product and its evolution in a simple and uncomplicated way, using the user experience as the basis for everything.

Our Advantages

Journey

Everything starts with the user experience! Right from the start you will be able to identify your users and understand:

  • The objective of each audience/persona
  • The user journey of each audience
  • And how they interact with each other!

Backlog

We have a new way on how to manage the backlog. No, we don't have lists, we have a map! You can view your epics/modules and features/user stories according to user experience:

  • Each part of the backlog is directly related to each stage of the user journey
  • You build what the customer will use
  • The evolution of items is visual, indicated by colors, so that you automatically know what is ready, in progress and delayed, immediately understanding how the customer will be impacted

Deliveries

Define your versions or deliveries with just a few clicks!

  • Select backlog items with the mouse
  • Give a name and date to that set
  • Monitor by highlighting visibility on the roadmap or just filtering that delivery

Agility

Master agile management through Scrum!

  • Have an editable kanban board per sprint
  • See your tasks separated by functionality/user story
  • Have your iterative and incremental evolution in a simple and easy way
